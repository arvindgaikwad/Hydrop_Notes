# Horizon Notes: Expansion Brainstorm

This document serves as the master hub for brainstorming new features, target audiences, and long-term evolutionary paths for the application.

## Current Baseline
- **Identity:** "The Canvas Without Edges" (Luxury, premium, distraction-free).
- **Core Feature:** Infinite canvas note-taking on tablets.
- **Tech constraint:** Local-first, <10ms pen latency, eventual sync.

---

## Brainstorming Directions

### Direction 1: The Local-AI "Second Brain"
*Instead of just storing ink, the app understands it.*
* **On-Device Handwriting Recognition (OCR):** Not just converting to text, but allowing you to search your handwritten notes instantly.
* **Invisible Linking:** If you write "Project Alpha" in a note, the app's local AI automatically creates a hyperlink to your main "Project Alpha" dashboard.
* **Smart Summaries:** Tap a button, and the app reads your messy whiteboard session and generates a clean bullet-point summary.

### Direction 2: The "Pro Creator" Toolkit
*Pushing the canvas beyond simple note-taking and into professional creation.*
* **Infinite Zoom & Nesting:** Like a digital whiteboard (Miro), you can zoom out forever, allowing you to nest a massive project on a single canvas.
* **Asset Import:** Drag and drop PDFs, images, or even 3D models onto the canvas to draw directly over them (huge for architects and designers).
* **Vector Engine:** Your ink isn't rasterized; it's pure math. You can select a stroke you drew yesterday and change its color or thickness.

### Direction 3: The "Sanctuary" (Focus & Mindfulness)
*Leaning into the "luxury" aspect. The app becomes an escape from digital noise.*
* **Built-in Ambience:** A beautifully integrated UI for ambient soundscapes (rain, deep focus lo-fi, white noise) that plays while you draw.
* **Zen Mode / Focus Locks:** The app integrates with iOS/Android focus modes to block all notifications while the pen is active.
* **Flow State Tracking:** The app tracks how long you maintain uninterrupted writing/drawing and provides stats on your "deep work" sessions.

## 🚀 Advanced Feature Roadmap (The "Gemini Web" Blueprint)

### 1. Contextual UI: "The Infinite Scratchpad"
*   **Picture-in-Picture "Scratch Window":** Swipe from the edge to pull out a temporary, floating mini-canvas over the main page. Saves automatically to a "Quick Notes" folder without cluttering the main infinite workspace.
*   **Spatial Minimap with "Portals":** Toggleable floating radar minimap. Users can drop "Portal Hooks" (visual bookmarks). Tapping a portal smooth-scrolls the camera to that specific coordinate.

### 2. Low-Overhead AI: "The Infinite Brain"
*   **Zero-Click Shape & Math Solvers:** Lightweight on-device layout parser. Draw an equation, a tiny `[=]` button appears. Tap to solve or render a perfect vector graph directly on the canvas.
*   **Lasso-to-Action:** 
    *   *Lasso -> Create Task:* Turns handwritten scribbles into synced OS tasks (Apple Reminders/Google Tasks).
    *   *Lasso -> Flashcard:* Converts a handwritten concept into a digital flashcard for active recall.

### 3. Deep Linking: "The Knowledge Graph"
*   **Bi-directional Ink Linking (`[[WikiLinks]]` for ink):** Write a word, lasso it, and link it directly to another infinite page or a specific folder.
*   **Infinite Web View Embed:** Drag a live, interactive browser window onto the canvas. Write handwritten notes next to scrolling documentation or a YouTube lecture.

## 💰 Monetization Strategy: The "Free-to-Write" Pivot

> **Strategic Shift (June 2026):** We are abandoning the AdMob/Ad-supported model. Tablet eCPMs are too low, and banner/interstitial ads destroy the "premium, distraction-free paper" aesthetic required to compete with GoodNotes.

### Phase 1: The "No-Ads, Everything Free" Growth Engine
- **Model:** 100% Free. No ads. No paywalls.
- **Marketing Angle:** Sub-millisecond pen latency and zero server fees. 
- **Goal:** Crush GoodNotes and Notability on raw performance and price to build a massive, loyal user base (Target: 50,000 active users) via organic Reddit marketing (r/ipad, r/NoteTaking).
- **Signature Feature:** Highly functional Native Widgets (write a quick note directly from the OS home screen without opening the heavy app).

### Phase 2: Specialized Add-Ons (Future)
Once the moat is established, introduce high-value, non-restrictive Pro Add-ons:
- AI handwriting-to-text transcription.
- Specialized digital planner templates.
- Advanced cloud backup slots.
