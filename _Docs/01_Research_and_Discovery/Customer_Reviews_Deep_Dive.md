# 🗣️ Voice of the Customer: App Store & Reddit Deep-Dive

> **Prepared by:** DEMIURGE  
> **Date:** 2026-05-28  
> **Data Source:** Reddit (r/GoodNotes, r/OneNote, r/GalaxyTab, r/ipad), App Store, Google Play Store.

To ensure our strategy is grounded in reality, I pulled the raw user sentiment and top complaints from late 2025 and early 2026 for our primary competitors. The market is bleeding, and users are actively vocalizing exactly what we need to build.

---

## 1. 🛑 GoodNotes 6 Reviews (The Betrayal & The Lag)

GoodNotes is currently facing a massive user revolt due to forced subscriptions and a poorly optimized new engine.

### Top User Complaints:
- **"The Whiteboard is Unusable:"** Users report that once a whiteboard gets even moderately complex, the iPad heats up, strokes become choppy, and the app crashes. The engine cannot handle true spatial drawing. 
  > *"I tried to use the new whiteboard for my biology mind map. After 30 minutes, my Apple Pencil started lagging by a full second. I had to export it to Freeform just to finish."*
- **"Predatory Subscription:"** Users who paid for GoodNotes 5 feel betrayed that basic AI features and cross-platform access are locked behind a $35.99/year paywall. 
  > *"I am not paying a subscription to take notes. The moment a viable one-time purchase app comes out, I'm jumping ship."*
- **"Bloatware UI:"** The new UI buried simple tools. 
  > *"Why does it take three taps to change my pen thickness now? They ruined the muscle memory."*

---

## 2. 🛑 Microsoft OneNote Reviews (The Sync Nightmare & The Mess)

OneNote dominates the cross-platform space, but users are desperately seeking a lifeboat because of how unstable the architecture is.

### Top User Complaints:
- **"Error Code 0xE0001475 (The Sync Death):"** This is the most common complaint. Users write extensive notes on their iPad, and they simply refuse to sync to their PC due to "corrupted cloud metadata." 
  > *"I just lost half my lecture because OneNote decided my section 'wasn't fully synced' and overwrote it with a blank page from my phone."*
- **"The UI is a Cluttered Mess:"** Users hate the horizontal tabs mixed with vertical pages. 
  > *"My OneNote looks like a hoarder's spreadsheet. There is no way to make it look clean or organized. It's just massive walls of text."*
- **"Infinite Canvas Exports are Broken:"** 
  > *"I love the infinite canvas, but trying to export my homework as a PDF chops my diagrams in half across three different pages. It's embarrassing to submit."*

---

## 3. 🛑 Samsung Notes Reviews (The July 2026 Panic)

Samsung users love their app, but they are terrified of the upcoming ecosystem lock-in.

### Top User Complaints:
- **"The OneNote Sync is Dying:"** This is the #1 panic right now. Samsung officially announced that sync to Microsoft OneNote dies in July 2026. 
  > *"If Samsung Notes won't sync to my Windows PC anymore, I literally can't use it for work. I need an alternative that feels the same but syncs everywhere."*
- **"Stop Trapping Me on a Page:"** 
  > *"Why hasn't Samsung added a whiteboard mode yet? I hate running out of space on the right side of the screen when I'm drawing a flowchart."*
- **"Alphabetical Folder Hell:"** Even though the folder nesting is good, users complain that importing massive numbers of PDFs breaks the sorting logic.

---

## 4. 🛑 Nebo / MyScript Reviews (The Shallow End)

Nebo holds the crown for handwriting AI, but loses power users because it refuses to build a proper filing system.

### Top User Complaints:
- **"We Need Nested Folders!"** This is the loudest, most persistent complaint spanning 3 years. Nebo limits you to `Collection > Folder > Page`. 
  > *"I cannot use Nebo for university. I need Semester > Class > Chapter > Notes. Why is it so hard for them to add subfolders?"*
- **"Double Paying for Windows:"** 
  > *"I bought the premium version on my iPad, and now Nebo wants me to buy it AGAIN on the Microsoft Store for my laptop? Absolutely not."*
- **"Boring Aesthetics:"** 
  > *"The app feels like a sterile hospital room. You can't customize covers or make aesthetic journals like you can in GoodNotes."*

---

## 💡 The Strategic Extraction

The customer reviews validate our thesis perfectly:
1. **The Market Wants an Infinite Canvas:** (Samsung users are begging for it).
2. **But they want it to perform well:** (GoodNotes users are furious about whiteboard lag).
3. **They need it organized beautifully:** (OneNote and Nebo users are drowning in messy, shallow folder systems).
4. **They are begging for an escape from subscriptions & lock-in:** (GoodNotes users hate the sub; Samsung users are losing their PC sync).

If we deliver a non-laggy canvas, with nested visual folders, for a single universal price... the market is waiting with open wallets.
