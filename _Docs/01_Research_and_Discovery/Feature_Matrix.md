## ⚙️ 1. Canvas & Spatial Architecture

| Feature | Infinite Note (Ours) | Samsung Notes | GoodNotes 6 | Microsoft OneNote | Nebo (MyScript) | Noteshelf 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **True Infinite Canvas** | ✅ Yes (Bi-directional) | ❌ Vertical scroll only | ⚠️ "Whiteboard" mode | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes ("Boards") | ❌ Page-based only |
| **Single-Tap Rotation** | ✅ Core Feature | ❌ No | ❌ No | ❌ No | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Zoom Constraints** | Smooth (10% to 1000%) | Capped (10% to 300%) | Capped (10% to 400%) | Uncapped / Messy | Smooth, uncapped | Capped |
| **Page Boundaries** | ❌ None (Seamless) | ✅ Hard breaks | ✅ Hard breaks | ❌ None | ❌ None (on Boards) | ✅ Hard breaks |
| **Minimap Navigation** | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | ✅ Yes (Whiteboard) | ❌ No | ❌ No | ❌ No |
| **Dual Page Opening** | ✅ Yes (Split View) | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes (iPadOS) | ✅ Yes (Windows) | ❌ No | ⚠️ Varies |
**Strategic Takeaway:** We are the **only** app combining a true bi-directional infinite canvas with **Single-Tap Rotation**. OneNote and Nebo offer infinite space, but no rotation tools.

## 🗂️ 2. Organization & Hierarchy

| Feature | Infinite Note (Ours) | Samsung Notes | GoodNotes 6 | Microsoft OneNote | Nebo (MyScript) | Noteshelf 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Folder Depth** | ✅ Infinite Nesting | ✅ Deep Nesting | ✅ Deep Nesting | ❌ Rigid (Notebook > Section > Page) | ❌ Shallow (1-level) | ✅ Deep Nesting |
| **Dual-Page / Split-View** | ✅ Included | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ❌ No | ⚠️ Varies |
| **Visual Thumbnails** | ✅ Live Canvas Previews | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes | ❌ Text titles only | ⚠️ Basic grid | ✅ Yes |
| **Global Search** | ✅ Handwriting + Text | ✅ Handwriting + Text | ✅ Handwriting + Text | ⚠️ Slow / Buggy | ✅ Handwriting + Text | ✅ Text |
| **Cross-Device Sync** | ✅ Real-time (All OS) | ⚠️ Samsung Ecosystem | ⚠️ Apple-centric (PWA elsewhere) | ✅ All OS (OneDrive) | ⚠️ Manual Sync limits | ⚠️ Same Ecosystem |

**Strategic Takeaway:** Nebo's and OneNote's biggest weaknesses are exposed here. Nebo's folder system is too shallow for power users. OneNote's rigid 3-tier system is bloated. We will adopt the Samsung Notes/GoodNotes visual folder system while applying it to infinite canvases.
