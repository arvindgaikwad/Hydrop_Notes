# ⭐ Strategy Rating: Capped Lifetime Pricing Model

> **Prepared by:** DEMIURGE  
> **Date:** 2026-05-30  
> **Status:** APPROVED by Operator  

---

## 📋 What Was Decided

### Business Plan — Pricing Strategy Change

| Aspect | Before (Original) | After (Revised) |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Lifetime tier name** | "Lifetime" | "Founding Member Lifetime" |
| **Availability** | Forever, unlimited buyers | **Capped: first 2,000 buyers OR first 2 years** (whichever comes first) |
| **Price** | ₹1,999 one-time | ₹1,999 one-time (unchanged) |
| **Access duration** | Permanent | Permanent (unchanged for those who buy it) |
| **After cap** | N/A | Lifetime option **disappears forever** → subscription only |
| **GoodNotes warning** | Not mentioned | Added as a cautionary lesson |
| **Scarcity marketing** | "Founding Badge" only | Real scarcity — "Only 847 spots remaining" counter |

### Financial Model — Unit Economics Update

| Aspect | Before | After |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Subscription price** | ₹999/year (placeholder) | ₹499/year (finalized) |
| **Lifetime price** | ₹3,499 (placeholder) | ₹1,999 (finalized) |
| **Lifetime user cap** | Unlimited ❌ | 2,000 max ✅ |
| **Max server liability** | Infinite (growing forever) | ₹1,44,000/year (fixed, known) |
| **Upfront cash from lifetime** | Unknown | ₹33,98,000 (funds 23 years of server costs) |
| **LTV:CAC ratio** | 39:1 to 58:1 | 18:1 to 33:1 (more realistic) |

---

## ⭐ Overall Rating: 8.5 / 10 — Strong, Defensible Strategy

| Category | Score | Reasoning |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Financial Safety** | 9/10 | 2,000-user cap eliminates the GoodNotes death spiral. Server costs for lifetime users are pre-funded for 23+ years. |
| **Marketing Power** | 9/10 | Real scarcity ("Only X spots left!") is one of the strongest conversion tools in SaaS. It's not fake urgency — it's genuine. |
| **Competitive Edge** | 8/10 | You still get to say "pay once, own forever" in ads — which directly attacks GoodNotes and Notability. But it's time-limited, so you don't bleed money. |
| **Revenue Sustainability** | 8/10 | After Year 2, you have pure MRR (monthly recurring revenue) from subscriptions. This is what investors and acquirers look for. |
| **User Trust** | 9/10 | You're being transparent: "This is a limited founding offer." If you ever need to change pricing, you haven't broken any promises. |
| **Risk** | 7/10 | Main risk: if the app doesn't gain traction in 2 years, you've given 2,000 users permanent access for very little return. But at ₹499/yr subscription, even modest growth post-cap sustains the business. |

---

## ⚠️ What Loses 1.5 Points

1. **₹499/year is very cheap** — GoodNotes charges ₹2,999/yr. Even at ₹799/yr you'd still be 73% cheaper and earn significantly more per user.
2. **Template Marketplace revenue** is still theoretical — it's listed as "Phase 2" but there's no concrete timeline for when it ships or how much it'll actually earn.

---

## 💡 Future Consideration

Add a **₹799/year "Pro Plus"** tier between Pro and Lifetime that includes marketplace access + priority support. This creates a middle step and increases average revenue per user without touching the lifetime cap strategy.
