## 3️⃣ After Launch: How You Make Money

You have 3 income streams:

### Stream A: Subscriptions (₹499/year)
- Students pay ₹499/year (₹42/month)
- This is your **bread and butter** — recurring, predictable
- After the Founding cap, this is your ONLY income source

### Stream B: Founding Lifetime (₹1,999 one-time)
- Professionals pay ₹1,999 once, get permanent access
- **Capped at 2,000 buyers** — then this option disappears
- This gives you a big cash boost early on

### Stream C: Template Marketplace (Phase 2)
- Users sell templates, you take 30% commission
- Passive income from both subscription AND lifetime users

## 6️⃣ The Safety Net: Why You Won't Lose Money

### Your costs are tiny:
- ₹2,533/month = You need just **6 subscription users** to cover all costs
- Even if only 0.1% of downloads pay, you're profitable

### The lifetime cap protects you:
```
  2,000 lifetime users × ₹1,699 (net) = ₹33,98,000 upfront
  2,000 lifetime users × ₹72/year server cost = ₹1,44,000/year

  ₹33,98,000 ÷ ₹1,44,000 = 23 years of server costs pre-funded ✅
```

### Worst case scenario:
```
  What if only 1% convert instead of 3.5%?
  What if only 15,000 download instead of 40,000?

  15,000 × 1% = 150 paying users
  150 × ₹424 (net sub revenue) = ₹63,600/year

  Your costs = ₹30,000/year
  YOU STILL PROFIT: ₹33,600/year ✅

  You literally cannot lose money on this.
```
